About Us

Sahseh Platform

Sahseh Platform is an independent youth platform founded in August 2022, bringing together Yemeni youths from various governorates. We work voluntarily to raise environmental, social, and youth awareness in Yemen. We believe that youths are the driving force behind change, and through our programs and initiatives, we seek to empower them to actively participate in building a better future.

At SAHSEH, we organize conferences, training programs, awareness campaigns, and several activities targeting pressing environmental and social issues. We also strive to build local and international partnerships to enhance our goals and impact.

Through our commitment to core values such as sustainability, transparency, and cooperation, we strive to be a pioneering platform that contributes to formulating sustainable solutions to Yemeni society’s challenges.

Our Vision

SAHSEH Platform aspires to be a pioneering model in empowering Yemeni youths to become active community leaders. The platform aims to play a fundamental role in achieving sustainable development by raising environmental and social awareness and supporting initiatives that create tangible positive change in Yemen.

Our Massage

Elevate Yemeni youths’ environmental and social awareness to empower them to confront related challenges. This awareness-building will come true by providing high-quality educational programs, awareness campaigns, and innovative initiatives that contribute to building a better and more sustainable future.

  • Enhance environmental and social awareness among Yemeni youth.
  • Empower youth to contribute to developing solutions to environmental and societal challenges.
  • Organize programs and conferences discussing environment and climate issues with active youth participation.
